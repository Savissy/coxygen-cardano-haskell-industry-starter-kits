Soulbound Membership & Identity

CoxyInsure utilizes non-transferable Soulbound NFTs (SBTs) to establish a permanent on-chain identity for each participant. Unlike standard NFTs, these cannot be traded or sold, ensuring that reputation and history stay with the individual.
