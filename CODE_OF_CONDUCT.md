# 🛡️ CoxyInsure Code of Conduct

Like the broader open-source and blockchain ecosystem, the **CoxyInsure community** is made up of contributors from diverse backgrounds, including developers, designers, researchers, and users from around the world.

This diversity is one of our greatest strengths. To maintain a healthy, respectful, and productive environment, we ask all participants to follow this Code of Conduct.

This document is not an exhaustive list of rules, but a guide to help us build a welcoming and collaborative community.

---

## 🌍 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the **CoxyInsure project**, including but not limited to:

- GitHub repositories (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Community forums and chat groups
- Documentation and support channels
- Events, workshops, and meetups

Violations outside these spaces may also impact a person’s ability to participate in the community.

---

## 💬 Our Standards

### 🤝 Be Friendly and Patient
Be kind to others. Remember that everyone is at a different stage in their learning journey.

---

### 🌐 Be Welcoming
We are committed to creating an inclusive community for everyone, regardless of:

- Race, ethnicity, or nationality  
- Gender identity or expression  
- Sexual orientation  
- Age or experience level  
- Religion or beliefs  
- Physical or mental ability  
- Socioeconomic background  

Everyone deserves respect and equal opportunity to participate.

---

### 🧠 Be Considerate
Your contributions affect others. Whether you're writing code, documentation, or feedback:

- Think about how your actions impact the community  
- Respect different perspectives  
- Be mindful of language barriers in a global community  

---

### احترام Be Respectful
Disagreements happen — that’s normal. But:

- Do not attack people personally  
- Focus on ideas, not individuals  
- Keep discussions constructive  

A respectful environment leads to better collaboration and innovation.

---

### 🗣️ Choose Your Words Carefully
We are a professional community. The following behaviors are **not acceptable**:

- Violent threats or aggressive language  
- Discriminatory or offensive jokes  
- Harassment or bullying  
- Sharing explicit or inappropriate content  
- Doxxing (sharing private information without consent)  
- Personal insults or derogatory remarks  
- Unwanted or inappropriate attention  
- Encouraging any of the above behaviors  

If someone asks you to stop, respect their request.

---

### ⚖️ Handle Disagreements Constructively
Different opinions are valuable. When disagreements arise:

- Try to understand the other perspective  
- Ask questions instead of assuming intent  
- Focus on solving the problem  
- Learn from mistakes rather than assigning blame  

Remember: collaboration is more important than being right.

---

## 🚨 Reporting Issues

If you believe someone is violating this Code of Conduct, please report it:

📧 **Email:** support@coxygen.co  

All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed fairly.

---

## 🛠️ Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior, including:

- Warning the individual  
- Temporary or permanent removal from the community  
- Restricting participation in project spaces
