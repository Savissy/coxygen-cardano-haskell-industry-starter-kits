📞 FAQ & Support

Section: 11. FAQ & Support
Use: Answers to common questions and where users should go when they need help.

This section answers common user questions and tells users where to get support when they need help using the dApp.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why can’t I connect my wallet? Usually the connected wallet does not match the approved account wallet, or the extension is locked.
  • Why is an invoice missing? It may already be funded, repaid, or still waiting for the UI to refresh after confirmation.
  • Why did my transaction fail? Common reasons include low ADA balance, wrong wallet, or expired browser session.
  • Can I undo a blockchain transaction? No. On-chain submissions are final once confirmed.
