👋 Welcome to the Invoice Financing DApp User Manual

Section: 01. Welcome
Use: Overview of the platform, what the dApp does, and how this guide is organized.

Welcome to the official user manual for the Invoice Financing DApp — your step-by-step guide to creating, funding, and repaying tokenized invoices on Cardano.

  • Connect a verified wallet and access your account securely.
  • Create invoice records by uploading a document and minting its invoice NFT.
  • Browse available invoices and fund opportunities as an investor.
  • Repay funded invoices and track all activity through the transaction tools.
Use the sidebar to move through the guide in order. Each page keeps the same layout so users can navigate the manual without losing context.

How to Use This Manual

  • Use the sections on the left to move page by page through the full workflow.
  • Add your real application screenshots inside each placeholder area after exporting them from your dApp.
  • Keep this manual in the same folder as the shared stylesheet so all pages preserve the same layout and brand look.

What Users Can Do in the DApp

  • Issuers can upload invoice documents and mint invoice NFTs linked to those records.
  • Investors can review listed invoices, inspect details, and fund open opportunities.
  • Issuers can later repay funded invoices, while all users can review transaction history and status.
