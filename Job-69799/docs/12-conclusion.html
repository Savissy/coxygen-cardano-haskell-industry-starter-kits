Section: 12. Conclusion
Use: A final summary of the dApp flow and what users should remember during daily usage.

You now have the full workflow for using the Invoice Financing DApp, from connection and invoice creation to funding, repayment, and transaction review.

  • Connect the correct wallet before performing any blockchain action.
  • Create invoice records carefully and confirm values before minting.
  • Review invoice details before funding.
  • Repay funded invoices from the correct issuer wallet.
  • Use history tools and logs to monitor the state of every action.
