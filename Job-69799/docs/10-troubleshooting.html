🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

Section: 10. Troubleshooting
Use: How to diagnose wallet errors, transaction failures, loading issues, and invoice state problems.

If something does not work as expected, use this section to diagnose the issue before retrying the same action.

10.1 Common Problems

  • Wallet does not connect: refresh the page and unlock the wallet extension.
  • Invoice does not appear after minting: wait for confirmation and refresh invoice lists.
  • Funding fails: check wallet balance and whether the invoice is still available.
  • Repayment fails: confirm the issuer wallet is connected and has enough ADA.
  • Transaction history does not load: verify the address format and session login state.
When troubleshooting a blockchain action, always check whether the transaction may already have been submitted before clicking again.
