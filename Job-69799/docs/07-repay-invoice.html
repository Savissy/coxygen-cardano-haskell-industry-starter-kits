✅ Repay an Invoice

Section: 07. Repay Invoice
Use: How issuers find funded invoices, repay investors, and move invoices into completed state.

Issuers repay funded invoices from the Issuer Funded Invoices section. Repayment closes the invoice lifecycle and records the completed state.

How Repayment Works

  • The app shows invoices that belong to the connected issuer and have already been funded.
  • Click Repay on the relevant invoice card.
  • Approve the transaction in your wallet.
  • After confirmation, the invoice is marked completed and should no longer appear in active sections.

What to Check Before Repaying

  • Confirm you are connected with the issuer wallet that created the invoice.
  • Verify the repayment amount shown on the invoice card.
  • Ensure the wallet holds enough ADA to cover repayment and fees.
